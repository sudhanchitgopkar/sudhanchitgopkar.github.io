About WikiVis

Ever wanted to see how Wikipedia articles connect to each other? Well, now you can! Introducing WikiVis - a new, elegant way to visualize data. In 3D! Enter a word and see where it takes you!

Enter the title of the Wikipedia article you are interested inand click the Visualize button.

Application Controls

  • p: Play/Pause visualization animation
  • a/d: Navigate between nodes
  • Enter: Go to article corresponding to current node
  • h: hide/show labels
  • Left Mouse Button: Click and drag to move visualization around
